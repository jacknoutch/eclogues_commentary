Vergil's Eclogues: An Online Reader

Read Vergil's pastoral poems in an interactive environment with commentary and language aids.

Vergil's Eclogues are some of the finest poetry in the ancient world. These ten short poems like painted miniatures: colourful, detailed pieces, and a window into a pastoral world.

This website is dedicated to making these delightful poems accessible in a way that makes them enjoyable to read.

When you're on a poem's page, click on a word you're not sure about to see relevant reading notes.
