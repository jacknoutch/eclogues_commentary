Vergil's Eclogues are some of the finest poetry in the ancient world. These ten short poems like painted miniatures: colourful, detailed pieces, and a window into another world. They reveal a pastoral landscape, rural but full of literary artifice.

This website is dedicated to making these delightful poems accessible in a way that makes them easy to read.

When you're on a poem's page, click on a word you're not sure about to see relevant reading notes.
